Connect the unconnected.

Nucleus is an Internet of Things (IoT) company, that enables omni-channel engagement and contextualized interaction for physical retail stores with their customers. Nucleus's IOT based customer identity solution unlocks the true capabilities of the IoT by powering dynamic relationships between people, things, apps and services. In Phase 1, Nucleus will focus on Offline Retail, Physical Security in Phase 2, and Interconnected Devices in Phase 3.

Meet Nucleus

Phase 1: 100M offline retail stores, 5B customers

Nucleus has spent 3 years in creating a proprietary sensor technology, that can uniquely identify anyone within a 200m radius, as well as sense temperature, pressure, motion, acceleration, and sound in the vicinity of the sensor. Using our ION sensors, retailers can identify and track customers’ entry into store, to paths taken within a store, all in real-time, while ultimately drive up in-store conversion using our universal loyalty program (ATOM tokens), as well as using our data to improve store layout and merchandise placement strategies.

ION Sensor

Nucleus’s proprietary IOT sensor that instantly identifies any customer within a 200m radius.

Orbit

Nucleus's blockchain based information storage, exchange and authorization layer. Connects Nucleus to global crypto economy.

Neuron

State of the art, deep learning platform that gleans data from ION & ORBIT, to create real time customer persona and relevant recommendations

ATOM tokens

Nucleus’s Universal Loyalty Program enables global exchange of loyalty rewards using our ATOM tokens

Proprietary Innovations

Nucleus has invested three years in technology research and development, to build a patent-pending sensor technology that overcomes all the drawbacks of existing retail technologies such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi beacons, to identify in real-time any person walking in the proximity of a Nucleus ION sensor.
