Connect the Unconnected

Nucleus is an Internet of Things (IoT) based customer identification platform that allows brick-and-mortar retailers to identify any customer who walks into their stores in real-time using Nucleus's proprietary ION sensor technology. Nucleus ION sensors don’t depend on any RFID, WiFi, Bluetooth, or even facial recognition technologies to operate. Nucleus's IoT-based customer identity solution unlocks the true capabilities of the IoT by powering dynamic relationships between people, things, apps and services. In its three phases of growth, Nucleus will focus first on offline retail, then physical security, and finally, interconnected devices.

Control & Monetize Your Own Data

Today, giant internet corporations monetize customer information through mobile advertizing solely for their own benefit, and have maintained exclusive control over said data. Nucleus shifts the power of this data monetization to the customers themselves, enabling them to take full control of their own data. The nCash token will be the currency for this data exchange, remunerating data providers and rewarding customers for sharing their data. This is decentralization in its truest and strongest form.

Meet Nucleus

Phase 1

Nucleus has spent three years creating a proprietary sensor technology that can uniquely identify anyone within a 200 meter radius, as well as sense temperature, pressure, motion, acceleration, and sound within the vicinity of the sensor. Nucleus has built the world's first IoT-based, contactless user identification system using a blockchain network to securely store customer data, while also building the world’s largest universal loyalty program using cryptocurrency (nCash tokens). Nucleus empowers retail stores with the intelligence of customer data, which until now was limited to online e-commerce experiences.

ION Sensor

Nucleus's proprietary IoT sensor that instantly identifies any customer within a 200m radius.

Orbit

Nucleus's blockchain-based information storage, exchange and authorization layer, which connects Nucleus to global the crypto-economy.

Neuron

Neuron is a state-of-the-art, deep learning platform that gleans data from ION and ORBIT to create real-time customer personas and relevant recommendations.

nCash tokens

Nucleus's Universal Loyalty Program enables the global exchange of loyalty rewards using our nCash tokens.

How does Nucleus work?

Using Nucleus, physical retailers can for the very first time, automatically identify every person walking into their stores and in real-time, offer a personalized shopping experience and recommendations, that were till date only feasible in the digital world.

Nucleus has developed the smart sensor network (patent pending), that can seamlessly identify every walk-in, relay that information safely and securely over Blockchain network to Nucleus neural network, overlaying this information with past behavioral data the store, retail brand or Nucleus has on the customer, and use it to offer shopping recommendations and personalized offers to the customer over their smart phone. These offers can either in the form of discount vouchers or nCash tokens that the customer can use to redeem discounts on their retail purchases.

Patents

O2O Attribution

  • Unique blend of telecom-enabled header enrichment technology and probabilistic device matching mechanism.
  • Identifies the customer journey across digital and physical channels.
    • Help brands to optimize their advertising spend as that they can divert their ad budget to the right channel.
    • Integrates Nucleus pixel with the brands' digital assets
  • Nucleus pixel identifies customer’s location, IP address, time of access as well as their mobile number.
  • Customer mobile number identified by pixel is mapped with mobile number read by ION sensor.
  • O2O attribution close loop the online and offline customer journey.

Dynamic Pricing

  • Nucleus’s proprietary algorithm reads the customer preferences and inventory movement together
  • Dynamic pricing algorithm tries to dilute inventory before it goes for mark down
  • The dynamic pricing is the combination of two different algorithms.
  • The inventory prediction algorithms would help identify the seasonality impact, markdown schedule, competition pricing impact, and slow moving products.
  • The customer prediction algorithms would help predict customers’ preferred products, possible basket size when they are in-store, and purchase intent.

ION Sensor

  • World's only presence sensor based on telecom grade technology
  • Identifies customers' mobile IDs on telecom radio signal
  • Ubiquitous coverage, reads mobile ID seamlessly from both smartphones and feature phones
  • Follows data security and privacy rules as per highly evolved telecom standards
  • Reads data from other sensor networks
  • Grants data access to external parties only with successful customer authorization
  • Effortless customer onboarding

Single Sign-On

  • Unique sign-on method based on mobile ID and mobile number
  • Authenticates user if the unique combination of mobile ID and mobile number is validated by telco
  • Eliminates the need to remember user ID and password
  • Saves cost of SMS OTP and backend process required to generate OTP
  • No dependency on network connectivity to send OTPs
