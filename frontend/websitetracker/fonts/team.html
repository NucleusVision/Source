Management Team

Abhishek Pitti, CEO & Founder

A Harvard Business School dropout, Abhishek has donned the entrepreneur’s hat to realize his vision for Nucleus. He is a seasoned management professional with a penchant for startups and entrepreneurship. In his decade-long professional career, Abhishek worked as an aeronautical engineer for Gulfstream and as a management consultant for Blackstone. Prior to Nucleus, Abhishek scaled his first entrepreneurial venture, LorvenSoft Tech, a company providing software services with operations in USA, Canada and India.

Martin Dudley, Retail Partnerships

Martin Dudley is a veteran in the retail industry, and brings close to 35 years of experience in retail sales and marketing to Nucleus. After heading the sales and marketing department at Levis Strauss for 16 years, Martin has spent over a decade applying his vast knowledge to IT, retail and financial SMBs in the USA in full-time and advisory roles.

Avinash Pitti, CTO

With a Computer Science Masters degree from the Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago and a Big Data and Social Analytics certification from MIT, Avinash has 14 years of extensive experience in SAP, machine learning, artificial intelligence, IoT, and blockchain implementations. His proven leadership and management skills across various industry verticals, including CRM, BFS, and oil and gas make him an important asset to Nucleus.

Brooks Atwood, Creative Director

Brooks Atwood leads all design initiatives at Nucleus. He holds an Architecture degree from Columbia University’s Graduate School of Architecture. In a career spanning over two decades, Brooks plies his trade in architecture, design and academics. Brooks is also a TV personality and was the host of the famous TV show "Trashformers" on the FYI network. At Nucleus, Brooks is responsible for overseeing and conceptualizing all design and innovation aspects of the company's products and services.

Nishtha Rohatgi, Director Telecom Partnerships

Nishtha Rohtagi leads the marketing team at Nucleus and currently drives telecom partnerships. As a sales and marketing professional with over 10 years of experience, Nishtha has held lead roles in product and marketing for some of India’s biggest mobile networks. Prior to joining Nucleus, Nishtha drove mobile advertising and monetization at Jio Infocomm, India’s largest 4G telco.

Sushmita Sinha, Head, Sensor Development

Sushmita Sinha heads the sensor program at Nucleus. A Masters graduate in electronics and communications, Sushmita has held telecommunication roles at some of India’s largest mobile networks. Prior to joining Nucleus, Sushmita held the role of Telecom QA at Crescent Systems and UAT labs.

Chandra Sekhar, Director Business Operations

Chandra Sekhar is the director of business operations at Nucleus. His domain includes everything from customer acquisition to creating value for the partners. A post graduate in Computer Science, Chandra is also a project management expert. As an certified Scrum Master, providing incisive business analysis is one of his strengths.

Praneeth Vangari, Program Manager

Praneeth Vanagari is program manager at Nucleus and is responsible for timely project operations and execution. As a project management graduate and professional, Praneeth is a Prince2 certified project manager. Praneeth has over eight years of experience in project management and business operations.

Sanjay Bisen, Director, Sensor Development

Sanjay Bisen is the Director of Sensor Development at Nucleus. He is an electronics graduate and professional who has had multiple entrepreneurial stints in electronics, software and telecommunications. Prior to Nucleus, Sanjay plied his 10 years of experience in the telecommunications and IT fields.

Bryan Lemster, Smart Contracts/Lead Developer

Brian has over eight years of experience precisely executing technology ideas, focusing on creation of custom social networks, large retail sites, native iOS apps and Android apps. He graduated from USC with a degree in Computer Science and Business.

Investors

CS Rao, Former President Reliance Communications Founder & CEO, Quadgen

Sriram Viswanathan, Co-Founder of Intel Capital Founding Managing Partner at IndusAge Global Capital

Darshan Patel, India's Entrepreneur of the Year 2016, Marketer of the Year 2015

Raj Mishra, Founder and Chief Investment officer, Indea Capital, Investment Banker with 20+ years experience

Raj Mishra is the founder and CEO of Indea Capital, a private investment firm. Indea Capital takes a long-term approach to investing in public and private equities globally. Previously, Raj has set up and run one of the earliest hedge funds investing in India during 2003-2015. Indea invested over US$ 1 billion on behalf of families and endowments. Prior to founding Indea Capital, Mr. Mishra worked in a senior capacity for a number of investment banks in Singapore, Hong Kong, Mumbai and New York.

Reliance Capital, US$ 60.12B Fund, 20M Customers

IndusAge

Since its inception, IndusAge has invested in 15 successful startups, including Nucleus. Their focus areas are telecommunications, AI, and IoT.
