Connect the unconnected

Founded in 2014 at Harvard University, Nucleus is an IoT based, contactless identification solution, that captures and provides previously unaccessed data to retailers and other ‘brick and mortar’ businesses through its proprietary blockchain and real time sensor technology.

Our proprietary IoT sensor technology doesn’t depend on any RFID, WiFi Bluetooth, or even facial recognition technologies to operate.

Meet Nucleus

Nucleus is composed of several technology layers, which together power our next generation platform.
Nucleus technology is a function of four primary components: ION, ORBIT, NEURON & nCASH

ION Sensor

Nucleus’s proprietary ION sensor network, can uniquely identify and sense temperature, pressure, motion, acceleration and sound within the vicinity of the sensor.

Orbit

Nucleus’s blockchain platform, Orbit, is the foundation over which customer identities, customer data and the nCash tokens flow accurately and securely between all involved parties and systems.

Neuron

Nucleus’s analytics engine, Neuron, leverages the strength of deep learning, blockchain and IoT to build a state-of-the-art intelligence and recommendation layer.

nCash tokens

The nCash token is our cryptocurrency, which powers transactions across the Nucleus ecosystem. Consumers can fully control and monetize their own data in the form of nCash.

How does Nucleus work?

Using Nucleus, physical retailers can for the very first time, automatically identify every person walking into their stores and in real-time, offer a personalized shopping experience and recommendations, that were till date only feasible in the digital world.

Nucleus has developed the smart sensor network (patent pending), that can seamlessly identify every walk-in, relay that information safely and securely over Blockchain network to Nucleus neural network, overlaying this information with past behavioral data the store, retail brand or Nucleus has on the customer, and use it to offer shopping recommendations and personalized offers to the customer over their smart phone. These offers can either in the form of discount vouchers or nCash tokens that the customer can use to redeem discounts on their retail purchases.

Proprietary Innovations

Nucleus has invested three years of technology research and development to build a patent-pending sensor technology that solves all the shortcomings of existing retail technologies such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi beacons, identifying any person walking in proximity of a Nucleus ION sensor in real-time.
